Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about navigating the VeriPass system and submitting your documents.

VeriPass is an online Transaction Processing System (TPS) designed to eliminate physical queues. It allows students to submit, track, and verify OJT applications, organizational papers, and academic requirements entirely online.

You can check the live status of your submissions on your Dashboard or the "Document" tab. Statuses include Submitted, Processing, Valid (Approved), Incomplete, and Missing. If a document is flagged as incomplete, you will be able to view remarks from the office detailing what needs to be fixed.

Currently, the system supports standard document and image formats, including PDF (.pdf), Microsoft Word (.docx), and JPEG (.jpg/.jpeg). Ensure your files are clear and readable before uploading.

The system dynamically calculates the estimated waiting time by tracking the average processing speed of the specific department you submitted to (e.g., if an office processes 12 transactions per hour, the system adjusts your wait time accordingly).

If the status is still marked as "Submitted," you may have the option to retract it. However, once an office begins "Processing" the file, you must wait for their evaluation to finish before making changes.
