Skip the Line. Verify Online.

VeriPass is a specialized Transaction Processing System (TPS) designed to streamline the validation of OJT applications, organizational papers, and academic requirements.

🚫

The Problem We Solve

Waiting in long queues only to discover your documents have incomplete or invalid information wastes valuable time. Physical submissions lead to inefficiencies, rescheduled appointments, and unnecessary paper consumption.

🌍

Our Mission & Goal

We aim to bridge the gap in convenience by allowing consultations and verifications to happen at home. This saves students travel expenses and meals while significantly reducing our collective carbon footprint.

⚡

How It Works

VeriPass automatically tracks processed transactions to compute office efficiency (e.g., 12 transactions per hour). This allows us to provide students with accurate estimated waiting times and live document statuses while in the virtual queue.
